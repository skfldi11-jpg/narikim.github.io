# Design Toward the Future of Learning for ALL

I am a doctoral candidate in Second Language Studies at Michigan State University.  
My work examines language teacher education, teacher emotions and identity, and critical approaches to GenAI in multilingual education, particularly in Global South contexts.

![Research agenda](/assets/images/research-agenda.png){: .align-center }

I focus on three research strands that connect theory, classroom design, and partnerships with multilingual learners.
